**Freak Alley VR Experience**

---

This is a VR experience for walking through Freak Alley.  Freak Alley is an  open air art gallery located in Boise, Idaho.


---

Tools used:

* 360 go pro camera
* Occulus qwest 2, 3, and pro
* Unity C#
* Blender
* Adobe Premiere Pro
* CoDeck - Kanban board
* Voices were recorded on Iphones
